How it Works

This calculator uses the RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) scale combined with your bodyweight for weighted calisthenics.

In movements like Pull-ups and Dips, your body is part of the load. To find your true max, we calculate:
(Added Weight + Bodyweight) / Intensity% = Total Max

Metrics Guide

  • 1RM: The maximum weight you can lift for a single repetition.
  • RPE 10: Maximum effort. No reps left in the tank.
  • RPE 9: 1 rep left in reserve.
  • RPE 8: 2 reps left in reserve.
