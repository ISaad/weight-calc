Key Concepts

  • StreetLift: Competitive weighted calisthenics (Pull-up, Dip, Muscle-up, Squat).
  • 1RM (One Rep Max): The maximum weight you can lift for one repetition.
  • RIR (Reps In Reserve): How many more reps you could have done with good form. (e.g., RIR 2 means you stopped 2 reps before failure).
  • RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion): 1-10 scale of intensity. 10 is max effort (RIR 0).
